Behind The Program

Every goal deserves a real coach.

No theory-only coaching here. Every program is built from what's actually worked, for competitive powerlifters and strongman athletes chasing a total, and just as much for people who've never stepped in a gym and don't plan to compete. Justin and Helene have spent over a decade competing at the highest levels of their disciplines, but most of the people they coach are simply trying to lose weight, move better, build real strength, and take control of their health. That experience under real competition pressure shapes every program, whether the goal is a national qualifying total, a podium at a strongman show, or simply feeling stronger and more capable day to day. Whatever brought you in the door, the approach is the same: technique first, consistency second, and a coach who's actually done the work themselves.

Justin holds the heaviest raw total in Canadian powerlifting history and has the 29th heaviest raw total ever recorded in the world, across all weight classes. He is also ranked 9th all-time in the 308lb weight class globally. His path to the top didn't start on the platform. At 17 years old, Justin was the Canadian National Track Cycling Champion before transitioning to powerlifting and earning his WPC World Junior Championship in 2016. That athletic foundation shaped everything about how he coaches: movement quality first, patient progression second, and recovery treated as seriously as the training itself.

Justin coaches the way he competes: methodically. Every lifter starts with an honest assessment of where they are, where the weak points actually are, and what's realistic in the timeline they've got. He's spent over a decade on the platform himself, which means programming decisions come from lived experience under the bar, not just theory. Whether the goal is a first meet or a national qualifying total, the process doesn't change: build the base, fix what's breaking down first, and let the numbers follow the work.

Our Approach

Coaching is the work, not the workout.

01
Efficiency first. We start by teaching you to move the way your body was designed to move. Before load, before intensity, before anything else, the pattern has to be right.
02
A pattern worth loading. Once efficiency improves, we find the movement that gives you the most back: strength, pain-free training, and a foundation that can actually be built on.
03
Enjoy the process. When you move well and feel strong, training stops being a chore. That shift changes everything. Effort goes up, consistency follows, and the work becomes something you want to do.
04
Train harder, train smarter. When you enjoy training, you push harder. That extra effort compounds over time in a way that forced motivation never does.
05
The results come. With the right movement, a loadable pattern, genuine enjoyment, and consistent effort in place, the results aren't a matter of if. They're a matter of when.

In-Person Training

Train with us at Just Be Strong, Niagara Falls.

There's a reason elite athletes still train with a coach in person. Real-time feedback is irreplaceable. In-person training with Justin or Helene means hands-on coaching at Just Be Strong in Niagara Falls: live technique correction, session-by-session programming adjustments, and a coach who can see exactly what's happening and fix it on the spot.
